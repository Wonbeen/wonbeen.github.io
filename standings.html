SKIDDERS
CHAMPIONSHIP

Skidders Championship is where both rookie and professional skateboarders
participate to improve their skateboarding career. It's great opportunity
for rookie skateboarders to get recognition from professional skateboarding team
and sponsors to begin their career as a professional skateboarder.
For professional skateboarders, it's a great opportunity to expand their influence
in the skateboarders' community and in the world.

WORLD
CHAMPIONSHIP

The world championship is a world-wide contest where skaters from all around the world gather and form teams and compete against each other. 4 skaters from each country form one team and scores are determined by the number of tricks, their complexity and success rate. The top country with the most scores is named world champion of the year.
