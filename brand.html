SKIDDERS: Skateboarders' Society

SKIDDERS was published in October 2019 and is currently managed by Joon Hyup Lee and Wonbeen Kim of Bugil Academy GLP. The name 'SKIDDERS' was originated from the fact that there is no skater who has not experienced a single failure. We are planning to expand our business to apparell and used skateboard market to meet the demand of boarders in our community.
